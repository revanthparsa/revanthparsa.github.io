I’m a Data Analyst at [BCG X](https://www.bcg.com/x/), working on applied machine learning and analytics for large-scale financial and consumer businesses.

My work focuses on building predictive and econometric models that directly influence business decisions. At BCG, I’ve developed credit risk and profitability models for a leading South Asian private bank and price elasticity models for a £1B+ European services provider, translating behavioral and transactional data into production-ready insights.

Previously at [Dhurin](https://dhurin.in/), I built recovery propensity and multi-class classification models for credit collections, achieving 3.5×–5× lift in top deciles. I also migrated legacy SAS pipelines to PySpark on AWS and automated ML workflows using Airflow, improving performance and reliability at scale.

I hold a Master’s in Artificial Intelligence from [Indian Institute of Technology Jodhpur](http://iitj.ac.in/), where my Masters Thesis Project (MTP) focused on Multimodal Knowledge Graph Embeddings under the supervision of [Dr. Anand Mishra](https://anandmishra22.github.io/) and a Bachelor’s in Electronics and Communication Engineering from [Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati](https://www.iitg.ac.in/). 

My technical toolkit includes Python, PySpark, Scikit-learn, PyTorch, TensorFlow, and cloud-based ML pipelines on AWS.

Recently, I’ve started working on agentic AI systems, exploring tool-using and workflow-driven agents using Cursor to automate modeling, experimentation, and analytics workflows.
